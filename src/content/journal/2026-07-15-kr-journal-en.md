## Lessons from Outcomes

The trades that resulted in losses, such as those on 농심 (004370) and 에스티팜 (237690), highlighted the importance of managing the magnitude of drawdowns. The losses, while smaller than the best gains, represented clear instances where the initial thesis was challenged by immediate market action. These outcomes suggest that while capturing significant upside is rewarding, the discipline required to limit losses when the initial momentum fades is equally crucial for maintaining a positive overall expectancy <img class="emoji-char" src="/characters/kr/11_thinking.png" alt="Kosho" />.

## Observations

The overall profit factor of 1.10 indicates that the gains slightly outweighed the losses across this sample of trades. The positive average win versus average loss ratio suggests that when trades do succeed, they tend to capture meaningful moves. This pattern is something to observe when assessing the relative strength of directional conviction versus the frequency of successful entries <img class="emoji-char" src="/characters/kr/12_relieved.png" alt="Kosho" />.
