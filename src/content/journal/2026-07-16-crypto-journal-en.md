The notable wins, such as the gains on PYR and ONDO, suggest periods where directional conviction aligned with market movement. The frequency of trades, coupled with the negative expectancy, points to a challenging regime where the cumulative impact of smaller losses is eroding gains from larger wins <img class="emoji-char" src="/characters/crypto/02_sad.png" alt="Bengal" />.

## Lessons from Outcomes

The trades that resulted in losses, particularly those involving FLOCK and GNO, indicate sensitivity to minor shifts in momentum that were not fully accounted for in the execution framework. This suggests a need for greater caution when extrapolating from single-asset performance across a high-turnover environment.
