A pattern of high volume accompanying significant price swings is evident. For instance, names like 리가켐바이오 and SK showed substantial positive price movement alongside elevated volume, while others such as 금호타이어 and 오늘이엔엠 experienced large negative price moves on high volume. This suggests that the recent trading interest was driven by specific catalysts or information flow related to individual stocks, rather than a unified sector rotation or broad market momentum. The presence of both large-cap names (SK) and smaller-cap names in the surge list points to attention being fragmented across market capitalization tiers.

Volume surges are purely an observation of where trading attention has concentrated this week and do not constitute a forecast of future price direction. The observed price action, whether positive or negative, reflects the immediate sentiment driving the elevated trading interest.
