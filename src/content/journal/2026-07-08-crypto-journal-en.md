The day's closed activity reflects a challenging risk profile. With a win rate of 13.1% and a negative expectancy of -3.12% per trade, the overall performance suggests that the average loss magnitude significantly outweighs the average gain. The disparity between the average win (+2.35%) and the average loss (-3.94%) is notable. While there were positive outcomes, such as the gains observed in EDGEX and MORPHO, these were insufficient to offset the larger drawdowns experienced, particularly with BLUE and RAD. ## Observations

The frequency of the negative outcomes, exemplified by the multiple losses in RADworks, suggests that the market structure encountered during these trades was unfavorable relative to the entry points. The magnitude of the losses, especially the -11.63% on BLUE, indicates that the risk taken on those specific assets exceeded the realized recovery potential. The data points toward a regime where downside capture was more pronounced than upside capture across the closed set.

## For Next Time
