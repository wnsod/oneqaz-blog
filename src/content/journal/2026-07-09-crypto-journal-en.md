## Observations

The spread between the average win and average loss indicates that while winning trades were generally more substantial than losing ones, the frequency of losses kept the overall positive expectancy modest. The performance metrics suggest that capturing larger gains on select assets, such as ZRC, provided a solid lift to the overall PnL, even when offset by several smaller negative outcomes.

## Lessons from Outcomes

The trades that concluded at a loss, specifically those on AVAIL, PARTI, and REQ, highlighted instances where the initial directional thesis did not sustain through the full cycle. These instances underscore the importance of respecting the magnitude of drawdowns; the losses were consistent in their negative range, suggesting that when initial momentum stalls, the risk profile warrants closer attention <img class="emoji-char" src="/characters/crypto/11_thinking.png" alt="Bengal" />.
